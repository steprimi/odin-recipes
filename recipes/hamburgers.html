Hamburgers

Description

Enjoy this simple hamburger recipe. Requires a grill.

Ingredients

  • Eight frozen hamburger patties
  • Eight hamburger buns of your choice
  • 8oz of bacon
  • Lettuce
  • One tomato
  • Red onion
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Thaw beef patties in refridgerator
  2. Slice tomato and onion
  3. Grill patties to at least 160
  4. While grilling, fry bacon until crisp
  5. Assemble burgers in desired order
