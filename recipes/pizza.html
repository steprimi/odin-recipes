Pizza

Description

A simple pizza recipe for conventional oven.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Frozen pizza dough
  • One can tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 16oz shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • Choices of toppings

Steps

  1. Smear butter on appropriately sized pan
  2. Place frozen dough on pan and allow to thaw and rise overnight
  3. Knead dough, stretching to desired form
  4. Spread crushed tomatoes or sauce atop dough
  5. Spread Mozarrella cheese atop sauce
  6. Add desired toppings
  7. Bake for twenty-three minutes at 400F
  8. Cut into slices and serve
