Lasagna

Description

Follow these instructions for a dubious dish. The result may be especially satisfying for armchair cooks, who tend lazy and rarely prepare their own food.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta noodles
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Haphazardly mix tomatoes, ground beef, pasta, basil, and Mozarella cheese in a Pyrex bowl that also serves as a plate
  3. Cook for one hour and thirty minutes
  4. Cool for fifteen minutes
  5. Enjoy
